Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

About Mind Companion

Mind Companion is a student wellness application designed to provide gentle check-ins, mindfulness nudges, and evidence-based micro-exercises. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, secure experience.

Data Collection

Mind Companion operates entirely in your browser using local storage. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store data on external servers
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Share data with third parties

Local Storage

Your check-in data, preferences, and progress are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Content Security Policy (CSP)
  • XSS Protection
  • Frame protection
  • Secure headers

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
